Lasagna

Description

A classic lasagna recipe with meat bolognese and bechamel sauce is full of flavor and made with delicious layers of meat, sauce and cheese. Lasagna is a complete dish that you can accompany with a light side dish, such as a salad, and a glass of wine. So you could put together a simple and romantic dinner or perfect for a meeting or special occasion with family or friends.

Ingredients(8 servings)

  • 3 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoon garlic,finely chopped
  • 1 cup celery, finely chopped
  • 1 cup carrot,chopped into small cubes
  • 1 cup tomato,chopped into small cubes
  • 500 grams of ground beef
  • to taste salt
  • to taste of pepper
  • 1/2 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 3 tablespoons flour
  • 11/2 cups milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • to taste salt
  • enough lasagna pasta
  • enough water
  • to taste salt
  • enough manchego cheese in slices

Steps

  1. For the meat, heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat and add the onion, garlic, celery, carrot, carrot, tomato and beef; add the red wine, tomato puree, thyme, oregano, bay leaf and season with salt and pepper; cook for 20 minutes until the liquid evaporates. Set aside.
  2. For the b√©chamel sauce, in a saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter and add the flour, brown for a few minutes and add the milk; season with white pepper, salt and nutmeg. Cook for 5 minutes until thickened and set aside.
  3. For the pasta, heat the water in a pot until it boils, add enough salt and cook the pasta sheets for 5 minutes. Remove and set aside.
  4. To assemble the lasagna, place a layer of pasta, a layer of bechamel sauce, a layer of meat and a layer of cheese, repeat the steps until the mold is full, finish with a layer of cheese and bake for 20 minutes. Cut and serve.
